# About

Norvegian farmhouse yeast with American Pale Ale hops

Brewed to show synergy between Voss yeast and Amarillo hops. 
Fermented hot to bring out its unique citrus esters - mostly orange peel and mandarin, complementing Amarillo's florar and citrus aroma.
This results in a sunny, aromatic pale ale that's bright, juicy and effortlessly drinkable, with clean, dry finish with touch of bitterness.
